My Biography

I was born near Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, on February 26, 2003. I am the younger twin sister of Kayla Hopple, and I was born 19 minutes after her!

On August 19, 2005, my family moved from Philadelphia to Wilkes-Barre, and it has been our home ever since.

Kayla and I graduated from Dallas High School in 2021, which is located in a remote town near Wilkes-Barre, where we have also made many outstanding achievements! Some of my most memorable achievements include:

  • Entering the spelling bee and winning third place both times
  • Being inducted into the National Honors Society
  • Participating in the chamber choir and band club in both middle and high school

In our first two years of Penn State Wilkes-Barre, we continued to capture more achievements and creating a more successful future before us. Some of the most memorable awards and moments I have achieved at this campus are:

  • I maintained a 4.0 GPA in both years!
  • In my sophomore year at Penn State Wilkes-Barre, I was nominated with the Ken Pollock Scholarship Award!

My Greatest Adventure

One of the best adventures my family has ever went on took place in June 2018, just after Kayla and I finished our freshman year in high school. We went on an educational trip to Arizona, where we learned about the Grand Canyon!

It was a five-hour flight from our Wilkes-Barre home to Phoenix, Arizona, and we stayed in a cabin-like inn, complete with an outdoor water park!

Not only did we visit the Grand Canyon during our week-long trip, but we also visited the Arizona DiamondBacks, which was our first MLB game ever!

Though we did suffer some jet lag after the flight back home, the whole adventure was worth it! It has been one of my favorite family adventures ever since!
